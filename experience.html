AlgoTrade – Voluntary Work (Apr 2025 – Present)

This project is a C#-based algorithmic trading strategy implementation designed to execute buy trades on financial instruments with robust risk management features including stop loss and take profit orders. Built as part of a modular trading bot framework, the strategy connects to MetaTrader 5 and utilizes real-time market data to dynamically enter and manage buy positions, ensuring only one trade is open at a time to reduce exposure and optimize returns.

Key Features:

  • Focused buy-side trading strategy that opens one trade at a time per instrument
  • Configurable stop loss and take profit levels based on pip distances for disciplined risk control
  • Integration with MetaTrader 5 API for trade execution, modification, and position management
  • Real-time market quote fetching for precise price calculations
  • Automated trade lifecycle management including entry, exit, and position adjustment
  • Support for trailing stop loss to lock in profits during favorable market moves
  • Logging and error handling to ensure reliability and traceability

Technologies:

  • C# and .NET for core logic and service orchestration
  • MetaTrader 5 API for trade execution
  • Dependency injection for service management and configuration
  • Asynchronous programming with async/await for responsive operations

Use Case:

This strategy is ideal for traders and developers looking to automate conservative buy-side trading with built-in risk management on currency pairs or other financial instruments. It serves as a foundation for further customization or integration into larger algorithmic trading systems.
