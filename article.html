My Developer Journey

Written by Mojalefa Mokgesi

Growth Through Real-World Experience

My journey into software development began with a passion for building and solving. After completing formal training in data science and cloud technologies, I sought out practical experience—leading me to collaborate on a live C# trading algorithm project.

The project challenged me to apply skills in object-oriented programming, logic flow, and real-time financial systems. It pushed me to write efficient, clean code while working as part of a small, focused development team.

With each bug fixed and feature implemented, I not only sharpened my technical ability, but also deepened my understanding of the development lifecycle—from design to deployment. This experience continues to shape the way I approach problem-solving, communication, and innovation in code.
