Becoming a Developer: Lessons from Real-World Projects

Published: May 26, 2025

My journey into software development hasn't been linear — it’s been an exciting mix of formal training and self-guided exploration. Volunteering on a live currency trading bot in C# has sharpened both my technical skills and my problem-solving mindset.

One of the biggest takeaways so far is how important clean code and logical thinking are when building something for real users. You quickly learn that coding isn’t just about making things work, but making them reliable and maintainable.

In this journal, I’ll continue sharing breakthroughs, mistakes, and the small wins that shape my growth as a developer. Stay tuned!
