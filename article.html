AlgoTrade – Automated Buy Strategy

Project Period: April 2025 – Present

AlgoTrade is a showcase of precision, automation, and intelligent decision-making in trading. The screenshot illustrates a powerful C#-based trading bot in action—executing buy and sell orders with built-in stop loss and take profit management, all in real time.

This strategy exemplifies disciplined algorithmic trading by maintaining a single trade per instrument, reducing overexposure while maximizing return potential. Integrated directly with MetaTrader 5, it responds instantly to live market conditions using dynamic price evaluation, asynchronous processes, and rule-based logic for every entry and exit.

Key Features

  • Focused buy-side automation with one active trade per symbol
  • Custom stop loss and take profit levels calculated in pips
  • Precision timing with real-time market quote retrieval
  • Direct execution and lifecycle handling via MetaTrader 5
  • Trailing stop option to secure growing profits
  • Robust logging, diagnostics, and error recovery

Technologies Utilized

  • C# and .NET for business logic and backend orchestration
  • MetaTrader 5 API for brokerage connectivity and trade control
  • Dependency Injection for decoupled, testable architecture
  • Async/Await programming for high responsiveness

Professional Value

This implementation is not just a technical feat—it represents the future of automated financial decision-making. Ideal for traders, fintech developers, or institutions looking for scalable and risk-aware strategies, AlgoTrade delivers both control and consistency in unpredictable markets.
